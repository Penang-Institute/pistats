Key Penang Statistics

Key Penang Statistics is a set of dashboards that visualise key socioeconomic data about Penang. Developed by the Institute’s Socioeconomics & Statistics Programme, it provides insights into Penang’s cost of living, trade performance, gender landscape, graduate employability, economic trajectory, income disparities, labour force statistics, state finance, and population trends from official government sources.
